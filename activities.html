Bouncing castle

Our resort is a place where kids can be kids. Parents can relax while your kids burn energy, make new friends and have fun.

Scubadiving

Scuba diving locations are great and our team is first class. There are nice relaxed dives, give you lots of opportunity to explore and see real beauty of the ocean.

Snorkelling

An easy way to enjoy the underwater beauty that surrounds our resort is to go snorkeling. All you need is a mask, snorkel, and fins. People of all ages and experience levels will take home wonderful memories about their time in the water with us. Our offers some of the best snorkeling in the world. It is an easy way to enjoy many of the shallow reefs, allowing for easy access. We provide two boat trips per day, one in the morning and one in the afternoon, which are included in the Snorkel package. Snorkelers may also be interested in taking the one day excursion to the Blue Hole which is regularly scheduled on Tuesdays (weather permitting) This is a full day trip with a picnic lunch at Half Moon Caye Natural Monument where can you see nurse sharks and myriads of brightly-colored reef fish that highlight the reef. Swim out a little further and you might see turtles, spotted eagle rays, seashorses, squid, and moray eels.
