Overview of Cool Sea Breeze Resort

Welcome to COOL SEA BREEZE Resort. An invitation for a truly unforgettable, lasting memory combining the very best of luxury and serenity.A short ride from Seychelles Airport, discover a paradise hideaway where luxurious seclusion meets exotic natural beauty. This verdant, beach-fringed island offers sophisticated pleasures on land and water - from genuine Filipino therapies in the spa to splashing, snorkelling or diving in the jewel-like Indian ocean and Seychelles most pristine lagoon. Designed to reflect Seychelles's indigenous culture, Cool Sea Breeze Resort is a beautiful, rustic island resort that benefits from a strongly defined sense of community and a relaxed, intimate atmosphere. Awash with Bougainvillea, Bird of Paradise and other native blossoms, this luxurious private island resort has an exotic village-like feel, making it perfect for families and couples for whom a social environment is an important part of the holiday experience.
