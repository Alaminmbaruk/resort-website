Accomodation

Awesome meals

Our resort has 11 dining options here. The options are also wonderfully varied, from top steaks at Butch’s Chophouse to our brand’s first Indian restaurant, Seychelles Club. And the variety makes for a great experience, giving the option of eating at a top restaurant every night of your trip.

Jacuuzi

For the ultimate hot tub (spa) experience, we have combinedluxurious hydrotherapy complimented with a 17" Pop Up TV, DVD/CD and integrated sound system

Lobby

When you arrive at your hotel, the doorman helps you with your bags as you enter the lobby. But instead of a reception area, you walk into a surreal campground fantasy—complete with a flowing stream and twinkling stars in the open night sky.

Showers

Our resort has western style bathrooms with Hot shower.

Swimming pool

Our resort offers private pools in the suites, private beach and a huge incredible outdoor pool. With the views to the bay and the bar integrated to the pool, Mexican cuisine and fresh seafood, the resort provides a five-star quality relaxation.

Telescope viewing

Our resort Astronomy Night takes place on the resort’s boardwalk every other Friday evening after sunset, and is led by an astronomer from the Seychelles-based Museum of Science and Industry, who uses stories and a telescope to leads guests on a solar journey of constellations and planets.

Wifi Enabled Rooms

The Resort is equipped with wireless Internet connectivity covering all meeting rooms, dining and entertainment venues, and guestrooms. The hotel uses Wi-fi technology helping conferences, training sessions and seminars to be taken to new heights of productivity. Wi-fi Internet service allows The Resort's clients to engage in online conferencing, web castings (streaming video/audio), interactive or remote training, hands-on product demonstrations and much more.

Wine bar

With a view of the pool area and a feel that’s somewhere between hidden gem and resort classic, our resort is a gorgeous place to dine and drink. Whether you prefer to be inside where you can feel the cavernous draw of the bar space, or out on the patio where the sounds and scents of the pool flow in, this is one restaurant and bar that has its experiences set and its desires fulfilled. You can head down to the cellar to see the wines or just hang out at the bar and have the services come to you.
